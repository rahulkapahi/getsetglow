ABOUT ME

Who am I ?

In the words of Ratan Tata “I don’t believe in taking right decisions, I take decisions and then make them right“ sums up my life. Having had the opportunity to travel the world and live in three continents I understand Power Dynamics, projecting the best image of yourself and Training programs which bring out your best from Communication Skills to Health and Wellness.

As a decade of my professional life was spent branding and managing a super luxury brand Mercedes BENZ, I have been blessed to have worked with the disciplined Germans, the crafty Britishers, the sporty Australians, the hospitable Arabs and the brightest of them all – Indians. This International experience along with an International Soft Skills consultant and a branding degree has helped me create customised training programs where the learners collaborate together in a nurturing environment towards specific Goals and outcomes.

I truly believe the best work you will ever do is to work on you. Its been overwhelming to have successfully consulted with the crème de la crème brands like Mercedes Benz DMEL, Louis Vuitton, Moet Hennessey, Da Milano, Gucci, Ask Automotive Private Limited and GD Goenka Schools amongst others.
